FAQ

What can I do at MSM?

We provide all the modern functionality for social networks. Here you can create your own community or join others. Quickly and conveniently communicate and keep in touch with friends and family. You can also run your own blog or page of your business or organization.

How do I get help?

If you need further assistance or would like to get in touch with us, please use the contacts to get in touch with us quickly.

Is your platform secure?

We provide a high level of protection and have a good reputation. MSM does not collect a lot of unnecessary information about you, as many large platforms do. We take a responsible approach to the security of our users, improving our systems all the time.

How do I start an account on MSM?

To get started, you should register on our platform and start using it. We have collected a lot of instructions, tips, and answers to frequently asked questions in our Knowledge Base.
